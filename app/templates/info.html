About [NAME]


Annual inflation in the United States was over 9% in June 2022.

This is the highest level of inflation since 1981.

With prices of everyday goods skyrocketing, you need to know what items are being most affected -- so that you know what to buy and what to avoid.
[NAME] is a tool that allows you to select what common household items are important to you, add them to your watchlist, and track how their prices are changing.

With [NAME], we're enabling everyone to stay in charge of their money.

Note: The source for all the price data for this app is commodities-api.
